Ski Touring

Ski touring, also known as backcountry skiing, is a thrilling blend of exploration and skiing. Unlike traditional downhill skiing, ski touring involves climbing up slopes using specially designed skis with attached skins to grip the snow. Once at the summit, you'll transition your gear and carve your way down through pristine powder, leaving behind the crowds and immersing yourself in the serenity of nature.
